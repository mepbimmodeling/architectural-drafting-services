What architectural drafting services do you provide for US projects?
We provide a comprehensive range of architectural services tailored for the US market, including 2D AutoCAD drafting, full Construction Documentation (CD) sets, 3D BIM modeling (Revit), and fabrication-ready shop drawings.
Do you follow AIA layering standards and US building codes?
Absolutely. All our drafting outputs strictly adhere to the National CAD Standards (NCS) and AIA layering conventions, ensuring compliance with regional building codes (IBC) and ADA requirements.
